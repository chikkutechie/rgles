## Introduction ##


rgles is a subset implementation of OpenGL/ES and EGL.
The main aim of rgles is to understand the OpenGL/ES pipeline.



## Features supported ##


  1. Projections
  1. Transformations
  1. View port transformations
  1. Depth test
  1. Direction light
  1. Basic texturing
